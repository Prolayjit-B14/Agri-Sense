# Platform Features

The AgriSense App provides a massive array of features tailor-made for modern agriculture.

## 📊 Scientific Intelligence & Analytics (v3.90)
- **21+ Forensic Diagnostic Charts**: High-performance visualization for Soil, Weather, Storage, Water, and Solar energy.
- **Dual-Line Correlation Engine**: Visualize cause-and-effect by linking environment variables (e.g., Weather vs. Soil impact).
- **Pure Telemetry Protocol**: 100% data accuracy; no placeholder values. Every chart point is anchored to a physical MQTT packet.
- **Stabilized Graphing**: Zero-lag, flicker-free rendering with disabled animations for industrial-grade precision.

## 📡 Five-Node Industrial Management
- **High-Density 2x2 Grid**: Real-time management of **Soil, Weather, Storage, Water, and Solar** hardware nodes.
- **Single-Line Forensic HUD**: Ultra-compact status readouts (Moisture ● pH ● NPK ●) for instant situational awareness.
- **Reactive Heartbeat Engine**: Automatic state-transitioning (Active → Stale → Offline) with human-readable issue logging.
- **Executive Hero Card**: Real-time system overview with weighted health percentages and network integrity metrics.

## 📹 Security & Vision
- **Animal Detection**: Integrated ESP32-CAM feeds that detect wildlife intrusions with automated alert logging.
- **Industrial Video HUD**: Low-latency WebSocket-based visual rendering optimized for mobile field deployment.

## 💧 Precision Irrigation & Energy
- **Actuator Overrides**: Clinical control of pumps and valves with real-time feedback loops.
- **Solar Energy Analytics**: Monitoring panel voltage, battery charge efficiency, and system-level energy load.

## 🤖 AI & Forensic Advisory
- **Crop Intelligence**: Real-time recommendations based on physical soil NPK, pH, and moisture telemetry.
- **Market Forensics**: Live price tracking and buy/sell advisories integrated into the diagnostic dashboard.
